Game Rules

  • Each player starts with full health and no energy charges.
  • The active player will charge his Pokemon's energy with each click of the charge button.
  • Charges 1, 7, and 12 are bust charges. Active player will lose turn and all energy charges for that round.
  • Hit attack button to unleash your Pokemon's energy at your opponent's monster.
  • Reduce their Pokemon's health meter to zero to become the Pokemon Champion!
